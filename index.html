About Me

Experienced, loving and responsible

Hello!

My name’s Jenny, and I’m a total animal lover. I would love to take care of your pets, whether you need somebody to drop by your house to check in on your cat while you’re on vacation, or need somebody to pick your dog up for a walk around the park while you’re stuck in the office.

I have been caring for pets in LA and NYC for the past 7 years, and have vast experience with many different breeds and personalities of both cats and dogs. I’m an equestrian, and I love going on long walks with dogs and spending time playing and cuddling with cats. I have also volunteered at shelters, and even worked as a veterinarian’s assistant in Thailand for a few months.

I have pets of my own (a cat and a rabbit), and have fostered dogs and cats, so I understand how important it is to dedicate time to your beloved pets. Send me a message, and let’s chat about how I can help you out with your pet care needs.

Hope to hear from you soon!
