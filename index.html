About Me

Experienced, loving and responsible

I have vast experience taking care of cats and dogs (many different breeds and personalities). I've also volunteered at shelters and worked as a veterinarian assistant. I love taking long walks (with dogs) and play and cuddle with them. I love all dogs and cats!

I've worked part time as a walker and a sitter in LA and NYC for almost 5 years now. I don't have a day job so I have a lot of time to dedicate to your beloved pet. I've worked in many different shelters with dogs and cats and I worked as a veterinarian assistant in Thailand with an American veterinarian for a few months. I'm an equestrian and go on many walks and hikes.

Hope to hear from you!
